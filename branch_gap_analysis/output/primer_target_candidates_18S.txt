                         NOTES
================================================================================

NF Interpretation:
  - NF = 1.0: Balanced representation
  - NF = 10.0: 10× underrepresented
  - NF = 100.0: 100× underrepresented (severe gap)
  - NF > 1000: Extremely underrepresented (critical priority)
  - NF = inf: Census-only, no reference genomes exist

Priority Rating:
  ⭐⭐⭐ = High priority (high NF AND high OTU count)
  ⭐⭐   = Medium priority (high NF OR high OTU count)
  ⭐     = Lower priority (moderate values)

Key Insight:
  18S eukaryotes show MUCH higher novelty factors than 16S prokaryotes,
  indicating that eukaryotic diversity is far more underrepresented
  in reference databases than prokaryotic diversity.
